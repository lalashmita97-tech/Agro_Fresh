Healthy Fat-Rich Food

Fats are one of the three essential macronutrients your body needs, along with proteins and carbohydrates. They provide the most concentrated source of energy â€” giving 9 calories per gram, compared to 4 calories from carbs or protein. Though often misunderstood, fats play a vital role in keeping you healthy, when consumed in the right form and amount. Fats are important for several bodily functions. They help in the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins like A, D, E, and K, which are crucial for immunity, vision, and bone strength. They also protect your internal organs by acting as a cushion and help regulate body temperature by providing insulation. Fats are also essential for brain development, especially in children. They are needed to build the protective layer around nerve cells and support hormone production, especially reproductive hormones.
