Protein-Rich Food

Protein are essential building blocks of our body. They help in muscle growth, repair tissues, boost immune health, and keep you energetic throughout the day. Including protein-rich foods in your diet is important for both kids and adults. Protein is one of the three major macronutrients (along with carbohydrates and fats) that our body needs to function properly. It is made up of smaller units called amino acids, which are often called the building blocks of life. These amino acids link together in different combinations to form various types of proteins that perform a wide range of functions in the body.
